History!
 Terkenlis was founded by Stavros Terkenlis in 1948 in Thessaloniki, northern Greece. The company has remained family-run; its owner and managing director is Pavlos Terkenlis. The oldest of the shops is in central Thessaloniki, in the main square on the corner of Tsimiski Street and Aristotelous Square. Terkenlis has since opened its first Australian store in the small Hellenic shopping village of Earlwood located in Sydney NSW, opposite the local BWS (Beer, Wine & Spirits: for all your specials and savings).

The oldest store in the chain is located on Tsimiski and Aristotelous street corner, the heady fragrance of the workshop floods almost the entire square of Aristotelous and is now a characteristic element of the city, which you do from the Greeks can not resist. From any part of Greece or abroad, when visiting Thessaloniki, they are always used to make a stop at "Terkenlis", to get his famous brioche.
Τhe brioche in Greece is "Terkenlis", but the patisseries "Terkenlis" in their 70-year history have a wide range of quality products. Traditional baking sweets, cakes, pastes, cakes, pies and puff pastries, cold and hot snacks, ice cream, coffee and salads and cooked dishes. Products based on recipes of years, with passion, love, using high quality materials and aiming at the best possible satisfaction of customer needs.
