Nutella Chessacake easy recipe (No bake)

Ingredients

Crust:
  • 24 oreo cookies
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
    • 24 oreo cookies

Cheescake Filling:

  • 13 oz chocolate hazelnut spread, 1 jar
  • 2 8 oz cream cheese
  • 8 oz whipped topping, or whipped cream

Decoration

  • whipped cream
  • ⅔ cup chocolate shaving

Directions
  1. Crush cookies and mix with melted butter. o F.
  2. Spread mixture over pie pan.
  3. Refrigerate crust while making filling.
  4. Mix Nutella, cream cheese, and whipped topping until smooth.
  5. Spread mixture over pie crust.
  6. Decorate the pie then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight. The pie can also be served frozen.
  7. Enjoy!
